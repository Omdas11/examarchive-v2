Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

ExamArchive ("we", "our", or "the Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our educational platform.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (used for authentication)
  • Profile information (if provided)
  • Account preferences and settings

Usage Information

We automatically collect:

  • Pages visited and features used
  • Upload and download activity
  • Browser type and device information
  • IP address and general location

Uploaded Content

When you upload question papers:

  • File metadata (name, size, upload time)
  • Paper details (code, year, subject)
  • Your user ID as the uploader

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Authenticate users and prevent unauthorized access
  • Process and review uploaded content
  • Improve user experience and functionality
  • Communicate important updates or changes
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database) with:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Row-level security policies
  • Regular backups
  • Access controls and authentication

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong, unique password for your account.

5. Third-Party Services

ExamArchive uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database hosting
  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting
  • Google Fonts: Typography (optional)

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required to provide the Service.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to:

  • Remember your theme preference (light/dark/AMOLED)
  • Maintain your login session
  • Cache user preferences

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers except for authentication purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your uploaded content
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at omdasg11@gmail.com

8. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account:

  • Your account data will be permanently deleted
  • Uploaded papers may be retained if already published
  • Anonymous usage statistics may be retained

9. Children's Privacy

ExamArchive is designed for university students (typically 18+). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
