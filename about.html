About ExamArchive

ExamArchive is a student-driven academic archive created to preserve, organize, and make university examination papers easily accessible.

We provide a structured, searchable, and community-powered platform for sharing and discovering exam resources — free for every student.

How It Works

1

Upload

Sign in and upload a question paper with full metadata — university, programme, semester, paper code, and year.

2

Review

Moderators review submissions for quality, accuracy, and completeness.

3

Publish

Approved papers are published to the archive — searchable and free for all.

4

Discover

Browse, search, and download papers organized by university, subject, and year.
