About ExamArchive

ExamArchive is a student-driven academic archive created to preserve, organize, and make university examination papers easily accessible — especially for students studying on mobile devices.

It solves a practical problem faced by students: scattered and unreliable access to previous-year question papers. ExamArchive provides a structured, searchable, and community-powered platform for sharing and discovering exam resources.

Our Mission

To make quality academic resources freely available to every student, regardless of their location, institution, or economic background.

  • Accessibility first — Resources should be easy to find and usable on any device.
  • Clarity over clutter — Focused on exam relevance, not distractions.
  • Community-powered — Students help students by sharing papers.
  • Built gradually, improved continuously — Stability over speed.

How It Works

1

Upload

Sign in and upload a PDF of your question paper with metadata (university, subject, year, code).

2

Review

Moderators review submissions for quality, accuracy, and completeness.

3

Publish

Approved papers are published to the archive, searchable and free for all.

4

Discover

Students browse, search, and download papers organized by subject and year.

How to Contribute

ExamArchive is a community project. Here's how you can help:

  • Upload papers — Share question papers from your university
  • Report issues — Found a bug? Let us know via the Support page
  • Request papers — Can't find a paper? Submit a request
  • Spread the word — Tell fellow students about ExamArchive

Development decisions prioritize usefulness, clarity, and reliability. ExamArchive is built with AI-assisted development tools, with all architectural decisions and content verification done by humans.
